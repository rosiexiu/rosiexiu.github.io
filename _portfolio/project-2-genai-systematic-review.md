## Overview

A systematic literature review examining how generative AI tools are being integrated into post-secondary education, with a specific focus on the distinction between **instructor-led interventions** and **unstructured student-initiated use**. The review addresses pedagogical implications, equity concerns, and design recommendations.

## Research Questions

- What are the documented effects of instructor-led GenAI integration on student learning outcomes?
- How does unstructured student-initiated GenAI use differ in its effects?
- What equity and access gaps emerge across different implementation approaches?

## Methods

- Systematic database search (ERIC, PsycINFO, Web of Science, Scopus)
- PRISMA 2020 screening protocol
- Thematic synthesis and comparative analysis
- Equity-focused analytical framework
