## Overview

Co-led a large-scale, cross-national survey study in partnership with WISE (World Innovation Summit for Education) and the USC Center for Generative AI & Society. The study examined K–12 teachers' perceptions, attitudes, and readiness for AI integration across **five countries: US, India, Qatar, Colombia, and the Philippines**.

## My Role

- Led statistical analyses of 2,000+ teacher survey responses
- Analyzed 40+ constructs including AI literacy, attitudes toward AI, ethics, and access equity
- Produced cross-national comparison summaries and data visualizations
- Co-authored international policy report published by WISE and USC

## Methods

- Large-scale survey design and deployment
- Structural Equation Modeling (SEM)
- Cross-national measurement invariance testing
- Descriptive statistics and data visualization (R)

## Output

- Policy report: [*Perceptions and Pathways to AI Integration*](https://www.wise-qatar.org/app/uploads/2025/11/wise-x-usc-perceptions-and-pathways-final.pdf) (2025)
- Preprint: [*How Students and Teachers Worldwide Are Adapting to AI*](https://doi.org/10.35542/osf.io/wr6n3_v1) (2025)
- AERA 2026 conference presentation
